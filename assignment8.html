Concept:

Toy car with movable wheels and a laser-cut license plate.

Original Plan


Source files:

    Car body gcode

    Kia logo (laser cut)
    "EV" license plate

Tasks:

    First I measured the steel rod and the wheels to inform the design of the body of the car. I traced the profile of the car and extruded it in Onshape.
    I test 3D printed a low-fidelity model to ensure the pieces would fit together. I also 3D printed a mold for my Kia logo, but it turned out poorly. So I instead printed a little box to use for the mold.
    I laser cut the license plate and Kia logo.
    I reprinted the body at a higher fidelity after modifying the design slightly.
    Assembly was a challenge has I had difficulty getting the support material off. Otherwise everything went smoothly!
